About Us

We are a group of students from KU Leuven dedicated to promoting reading and literacy in our community through book exchange initiatives.

Our mission is to make books more accessible to everyone and foster a love for reading.

This website is a course project created and built by Meihan Luo, Yiyao Jin, Zita Baronnet, Renfei Xu and Yuxuan Kang in May 2024, using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Bootstrap.
